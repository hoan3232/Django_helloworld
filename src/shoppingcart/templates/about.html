About Us

JONGLE'LIVRE

Coming to JONGLE'LIVRE, you will easily find the books you want as well as the latest and hottest books in the world. The bookstore currently has more than 100,000 books of different genres. From history books to science books, from domestic literature to foreign literature and countless books from other topics such as comics, novels,... you can choose and explore.

Our primary mission is to bring you good books at reasonable prices. Over the years, JONGLE'LIVRE has had a definite place in the hearts of every book lover. JONGLE'LIVRE is always a prestigious place to be remembered by everyone. Although we are small in size, we are always ready to support you on your way to finding knowledge.

Your love and support will be a great motivation for JONGLE'LIVRE to develop more and more.
